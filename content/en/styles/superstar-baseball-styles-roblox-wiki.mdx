Inspired by real-world hall-of-famers, the Legend style features a high leg kick and a massive follow-through. It is widely considered the best style for home run hitters. The Compact style is the gold standard for competitive play in 2026. It features minimal pre-swing movement, which allows the player to focus entirely on the pitcher's release point. ## Advanced Mechanics: Load, Contact, and Follow-through

To truly utilize the information found in this **superstar baseball styles roblox wiki**, you must understand the three phases of a swing. Each style handles these phases differently.

1.  **The Load Phase:** This is the initial movement where the batter shifts their weight back. Styles with a "heavy" load often generate more power but require earlier input.
2.  **The Contact Phase:** The moment the bat moves through the zone. The "Style" determines the arc of the bat. A "Level" arc is better for line drives, while an "Uppercut" arc is better for fly balls.
3.  **The Follow-through:** The animation that plays after the swing. While this doesn't affect the ball's flight, it dictates how quickly your character can begin running to first base.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Some "Legendary" styles have very long follow-through animations. While they look impressive, they can occasionally delay your sprint to first base on close infield hits.
