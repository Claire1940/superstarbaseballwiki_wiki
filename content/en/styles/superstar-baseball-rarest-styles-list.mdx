## Advanced Hitting Techniques with Rare Styles

Having a style from the **superstar baseball rarest styles list** is only half the battle. To hit "insane" home runs, you must master the mechanics of timing and coordination. Even with an Impact style, a "Late" or "Early" swing will result in a foul ball or a weak grounder.

### Timing is Everything
The game's engine calculates the point of contact based on the millisecond you click or tap. For a power style to activate its full potential, you want "Perfect" timing. This usually means swinging just as the ball enters the front half of the hitting zone.

### Ball Placement
You need to align your reticle (the hitting circle) with the center of the ball. Power styles like Combustion actually have a slight "explosion" effect upon contact, which can help propel the ball further if your alignment is slightly off-center, but aiming for the "sweet spot" is always the goal.
