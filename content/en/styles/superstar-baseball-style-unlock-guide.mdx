## Defensive Styles and Essential Traits

Unlocking a style is only half the battle. To truly optimize your player, you must pair your style with the right **Traits**. Traits are passive bonuses that trigger under specific conditions. For example, a catcher with the **Cannon Arm** trait is significantly more likely to throw out runners attempting to steal second base.

> **Warning:** Some traits, like **Brick Wall**, only activate when runners are in scoring position. Make sure your lineup is ordered to take advantage of these situational buffs.

### Key Defensive Traits to Equip

*   **Cannon Arm:** Essential for Catchers and Center Fielders. Increases throw velocity to bases.
*   **Brick Wall:** Penalizes the batter's contact window when runners are on base.
*   **Focus:** Increases the "Perfect" timing window for cleanup hitters (3rd, 4th, and 5th in the lineup).
*   **Bad Ball Hitter:** An offensive trait that allows for solid contact even on pitches outside the strike zone.
