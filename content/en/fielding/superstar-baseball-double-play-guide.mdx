## Understanding the Double Play Mechanics

In the current 2026 version of the game, double plays are heavily influenced by the "DEX" (Dexterity) and "SPD" (Speed) stats of your Second Baseman (2B) and Shortstop (SS). Unlike manual hitting, defensive plays often rely on the quality of the players you have created in the Develop mode. 

When a ball is hit into the infield with a runner on first, the game calculates the success of a double play based on the transition speed between the fielder who picks up the ball and the fielder covering the bag. If your players have low fielding stats, they may bobble the ball or throw a "rainbow" toss that allows the runner to reach first base safely.

### Key Stats for Infielders

| Stat | Importance | Role in Double Plays |
|---|---|---|
| **DEX (Dexterity)** | High | Determines the speed of the ball transfer from glove to hand. |
| **SPD (Speed)** | Medium | Helps the 2B or SS reach the bag faster to receive the lead throw. |
| **STR (Strength)** | Low | Affects throw velocity, though DEX is more vital for short-distance infield turns. |
| **Fielding (FLD)** | Essential | Reduces the chance of errors during high-velocity grounders. |

> **💡 Tip:** Always prioritize DEX when developing your Shortstop. A high-DEX SS can fire the ball to second base almost instantly, giving your 2B plenty of time to relay to first.

## FAQ

**Q: Why is my team not attempting double plays even when the ball is hit to the SS?**

A: This is usually due to low DEX stats or a lack of the "Double Play" logic skill on your developed players. Use this **superstar baseball double play guide** to refocus your development on Dexterity and specialized fielding trainers.

**Q: Does the First Baseman (1B) need high stats for double plays?**

A: While the 1B is the final part of the play, their primary job is to catch. Having a 1B with a high "Catching" or "Height" stat can help prevent errors on wide throws from the second baseman, but the speed of the play depends mostly on the SS and 2B.

**Q: Can I trigger a double play manually?**

A: In most modes, the fielding is handled by the AI based on your players' stats. However, in "Live Clash," your reaction time in selecting the correct base to throw to is the deciding factor. Ensure you tap "2nd Base" and then "1st Base" in rapid succession.

**Q: What is the best way to farm gold for trainer upgrades?**

A: Completing Daily Missions and participating in the "Daily Game" are the most consistent ways to earn gold. While selling players provides some gold, it is generally considered a secondary farming method compared to league rewards.
