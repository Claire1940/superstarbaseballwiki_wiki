## Advanced Pitching and Fielding

The defensive side of the **superstar baseball season 3 update guide** focuses on "Star Pitching." In Season 3, the AI has become much better at reading standard fastballs. You must vary your pitch location and speed to maintain a low ERA.

### Pitching Tips
1.  **The Slider:** Hold the joystick left or right while charging your pitch. This is particularly effective with Peach or Waluigi.
2.  **The Change-up:** Tap the button lightly. This lures the batter into swinging early, leading to easy ground-outs.
3.  **Star Pitches:** Only use these when facing the opponent's captain or when the bases are loaded. Stars are a limited resource; wasting them on "Grunt" players like Goombas or Shy Guys is a common mistake.

### Fielding and Base Running
Base running in 2026 requires quick reflexes. If you hit a ball into the gap, keep a close eye on the outfielders. Characters like [Nintendo's iconic roster](https://www.nintendo.com) have varying arm strengths. If a Pianta is throwing from the wall, do not attempt to take third base; their arm strength is high enough to throw you out by a mile.
