### Mastering the Fake Ball Pitch
To successfully utilize the Fake Ball, pitchers should aim for the corners of the strike zone. Because the hitter must guess which ball is tangible, placing the real ball on the "black" (the edges of the plate) while the fake ball drifts toward the middle often results in a swinging strikeout.

## Strategies for the 2026 Competitive Season

To excel in the current meta, you must balance your roster with high-average hitters and "gimmick" pitchers. While home runs are flashy, the 2026 defensive meta has become very efficient at catching fly balls. 1.  **Pitch Variation:** Do not spam fastballs. Use the "Low Change-up" to force weak contact.
2.  **Contact Hitting:** If you aren't using a power style like Combustion, focus on "On Time" contact rather than "Power" swings.
3.  **Fielding Awareness:** Always keep a finger on the "1" key (for first base) to ensure quick transitions after fielding a grounder.
