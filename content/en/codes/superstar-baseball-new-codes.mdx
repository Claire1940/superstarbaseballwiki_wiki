## Gameplay Tips for New Superstars

Once you have used your **superstar baseball new codes** to get your preferred style, you need to master the mechanics to actually win games.

### Master the Pitching Meter
Pitching isn't just about speed; it's about placement and timing. Watch the meter carefully and try to release the ball when the indicator is in the green zone. This reduces the chance of a "Meatball" (an easy-to-hit pitch) and increases the break on your curveballs.

### Batting Timing
Unlike arcade baseball games, Superstar Baseball relies heavily on the "Contact" vs. "Power" swing choice. If you have two strikes, switch to a contact swing to increase your hit window. Use power swings only when you have a favorable count (like 2-0 or 3-1).
