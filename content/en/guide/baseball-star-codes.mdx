### Player Training and Development

Don't spend all your CP on a single superstar. A balanced team usually outperforms a squad with one "carry" and eight mediocre players. Focus your training on these key stats based on the player's position:

*   **Pitchers**: Prioritize **Stamina** and **Velocity**. In the late-game stages of 2026, high-velocity pitchers are much harder for the AI and other players to read.
*   **Batters**: Focus on **Power** and **Contact**. If your batter has high power but low contact, they will strike out frequently despite their potential for home runs.
*   **Fielders**: **Speed** and **Fielding** are the most overlooked stats. High speed allows your outfielders to catch fly balls that would otherwise become extra-base hits.
