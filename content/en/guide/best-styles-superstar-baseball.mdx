## FAQ

**Q: What are the best styles superstar baseball beginners should pick?**

A: Beginners should start with the **Heat** or **Combustion** styles. These provide a solid balance of power and ease of use, allowing new players to learn timing without being penalized too heavily for missing the "sweet spot."

**Q: Is the Impact style better than Combustion?**

A: Technically, **Impact** has higher maximum potential for home run distance. However, **Combustion** is often considered better for most players because it is easier to use and much simpler to unlock in the 2026 progression system.

**Q: Can I change my style after I have selected it?**

A: Yes, you can switch styles in the main menu under the "Player Customization" tab. Note that style-specific experience does not always transfer, so it is best to pick one and master it.

**Q: How do I improve my timing with power styles?**

A: Use the practice facility mode. Set the pitching machine to "Random" and focus on identifying the pitch early. Power styles like Impact require you to be slightly earlier on the ball than contact styles.
