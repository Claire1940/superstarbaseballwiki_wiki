## Pitching Mechanics: The Art of the "Dot"

Unlike other Roblox baseball titles that use drag-and-drop mechanics, Superstar Baseball utilizes a "dot" system. Pitchers select a pitch type and then click a specific location within or outside the strike zone to "dot" the ball.

### Standard Pitch Types

| Pitch Name | Movement Pattern | Best Use Case |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Fastball** | High velocity, straight path. | Overpowering batters who swing early. |
| **Sinker** | Drops sharply near the plate. | Forcing ground balls and double plays. |
| **Slider** | Horizontal break away from the batter. | Inducing swings and misses on the corners. |
| **Curveball** | Large vertical and horizontal break. | Keeping the batter's timing off-balance. |

### The Shadow Pitch and Super Abilities
Advanced pitchers can unlock the **Shadow Pitch**. This ability creates a delayed animation where the ball appears to be thrown significantly later than the actual release. This "anime-style" mechanic is designed to confuse the batter's timing, often leading to easy strikeouts.

## Batting Strategies: Power vs. Contact

Batting in 2026 requires a mix of patience and "reading" the pitcher's dots. Players can choose between **Power** and **Contact** swings. Power swings result in higher-velocity "nukes" that are more likely to clear the fence for a home run, but they have a smaller timing window. Contact swings are safer and better for moving runners along the bases.

1.  **Watch the Release:** Pay attention to the pitcher's wind-up. Custom stances can hide the ball's release point.
2.  **Track the High Pitches:** Some pitchers attempt to throw the ball so high it leaves the camera frame. Stay calm and wait for the ball to descend into the zone.
3.  **Use Bouncy Ball Abilities:** If you have the Elastic style, focus on hitting the ball into the gaps. The unpredictable bounces often lead to extra-base hits.
4.  **Timing the Super Pitch:** When a pitcher uses a custom anime aura, wait an extra half-second. These pitches often have a "lag" effect built into the animation.

## Defensive Maneuvers and Baserunning

Fielding is just as important as hitting in Superstar Baseball. The game features a 7v7 format, meaning every player on the field must be active. 

### Key Controls for Fielders
*   **E (Dive):** Essential for stopping ground balls that are just out of reach.
*   **Q (Buddy Jump):** A specialized move used to rob home runs at the wall with the help of a teammate.
*   **Auto-Throw:** The game allows for quick targeting of bases, but manual aiming is often faster for experienced players.

Baserunning is equally intense. Players can use **Rocket Boost** abilities to gain a temporary burst of speed when rounding second or third. However, defenders in 2026 often have "cannon arms," meaning a single hesitation can result in a tag-out. Always slide into the base to minimize your hit box and avoid being tagged.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not leave your base too early. Pitchers can quickly throw to any bag to pick off runners who are "juking" or lead-off too far.
